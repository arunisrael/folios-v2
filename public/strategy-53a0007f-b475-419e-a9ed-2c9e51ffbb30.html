   - Price-to-Free-Cash-Flow (prefer < 12)
   - Debt-to-Equity ratio (prefer < 0.5)
   - Return on Equity (prefer > 10%)

   - Recent price declines (20%+ from 52-week highs)
   - Market overreaction to short-term issues
   - Strong fundamentals despite poor recent performance
   - Asset backing (tangible book value support)

3. **Quality Assessment**: Ensure business quality despite low valuation
   - Sustainable competitive advantages
   - Consistent earnings history (5+ years)
   - Strong management track record
   - Growing or stable market share
