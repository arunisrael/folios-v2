Focus on small undiscovered companies using local knowledge and industry expertise. Look for companies with sustainable growth stories before institutional discovery.

Focus on systematic application of strategy principles for consistent results.

ANALYSIS REQUIREMENTS:
1. **Growth Metrics**: Focus on accelerating business expansion

   - Earnings growth (prefer 20%+ annually)

   - Operating margin expansion
   - Market share gains
   - Customer/user growth rates

2. **Innovation & Competitive Position**: Assess sustainable growth drivers
   - Product/service innovation pipeline
   - Technology advantages or network effects

   - Cash flow generation from operations
   - Capital efficiency (ROIC > 15%)
   - Organic vs acquisition-driven growth

   - Scalability of business model
